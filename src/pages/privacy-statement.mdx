This is my personal space. Everything here — the photographs, generative art, writings, notes, and all other creative works — is mine. This statement covers both how I handle your privacy as a visitor and how I expect my content to be treated.

## Content Protection

All photographs, artwork, generative art, illustrations, writings, and other creative works published on this site are personal works by Ahmad Atallah. They are shared here for creative expression, not for commercial use.

**You may not:**

- Use any content from this site in advertisements, marketing, or promotional materials
- Use any content for commercial or business purposes
- Scrape, harvest, or bulk-download images or text for any purpose
- Use any content to train AI models, machine learning systems, or datasets
- Reproduce, redistribute, or republish content without explicit written permission

**You may:**

- Share links to this site
- Reference or quote small excerpts with proper attribution and a link back
- View and enjoy the work as intended

If you'd like to use any of my work, ask first at [hi@atallahsan.cc](mailto:hi@atallahsan.cc).

## What I Don't Collect

This site is built with privacy in mind. I don't use:

- Analytics or tracking (no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
- Cookies for tracking behavior
- User accounts or registration
- Data collection forms

## What May Be Collected

**Server Logs**: The web server automatically logs basic technical information (IP addresses, browser types, page requests) for security purposes. This data isn't linked to your identity or shared with anyone.

**Email Contact**: If you email me, I keep our correspondence to reply to you. Your email address is only used for that purpose.

**Accessibility Settings**: Your accessibility preferences are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to my servers.

## Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information. Contact me at [hi@atallahsan.cc](mailto:hi@atallahsan.cc).

This site links to external websites with their own privacy policies. I'm not responsible for their practices.

## Security

- **HTTPS**: All communications are encrypted
- **Static site**: No server-side processing, no databases, reduced attack surface
- **No third-party trackers**: Your visit stays between you and this site
